Welcome to the WAISN SMS Alert System!


The WAISN SMS Alert System permits users to subscribe to receiving SMS alerts by texting to the WAISN SMS System phone number. After they are subscribed, this website can be used to send two types of notifications:

  • Alert Notifications: after an event has been confirmed, use an alert notification to send out the address of the event.
  • Follow Up Notifications: after an event has terminated, use a follow up notification to send out information about the event and follow up details.

The forms for both of these notifications can be found in the top navigation bar under Alert Form and Follow Up Form, respectively. The directions are as follows for both notifications:

  1. Go to the desired notification form
  2. Fill out the form
  3. Hit Submit
  4. All the people subscribed to receive notifications to receive the message in their desired language
